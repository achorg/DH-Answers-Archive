Yes, the learning curve is steeper, but the power of Drupal and its wonderful modules ecosystem make it worthwhile, particularly if you're trying to give people skills that will enable them to make sophisticated sites later. The basic concepts are pretty easy and installing the Administrator Menu module makes site navigation when adding content much, much simpler. Then, once they have basic navigation and content adding concepts down, you can highlight the most important modules like CCK and Views.</p> <p>Of course, I'm partial to Drupal anyway.
