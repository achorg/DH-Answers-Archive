First, as Julie notes, what you plan to do with the video; second, what type of media you are saving to (e.g., hard drive, dvd, etc); third, what type of software you will most likely use if you go back to that video; and fourth, what type of media you use for shooting (e.g., DV tape, memory card, miniDVD, etc.)</p> <p>Personally, I still prefer to shoot and store my video on DV Tape. All digital formats are tremendous memory hogs and it's very easy to fill up terabyte drives with video footage you may or may not go back to.
