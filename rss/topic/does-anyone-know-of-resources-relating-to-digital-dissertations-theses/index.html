Their entry in the NEH database is <a href="https://securegrants.neh.gov/publicquery/main.aspx?f=1&amp;gn=HD-51561-12" rel="nofollow">https://securegrants.neh.gov/publicquery/main.aspx?f=1&amp;gn=HD-51561-12</a> and the video of their lightning round talk at the NEH project directors meeting where they describe their project is <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ahuULHlcsoU#t=9m27s" rel="nofollow">http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ahuULHlcsoU#t=9m27s</a> .
