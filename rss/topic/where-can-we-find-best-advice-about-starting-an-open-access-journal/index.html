So does Jan Velterop's <a href="http://www.soros.org/openaccess/scholarly_guide.shtml">Guide to Open Access Publishing and Scholarly Societies</a>.</p> <p>On the mechanics of electronic publishing generally, try Sally Morris's <a href="http://www.inasp.info/file/179/getting-started-in-electronic-publishing.html">Getting Started in Electronic Journal Publishing</a>.</p> <p>With regard to software, the open-source choice is currently <a href="http://pkp.sfu.ca/?q=ojs">Open Journal Systems</a>. Before you decide to download and install it, CHECK WITH YOUR LIBRARY (and have your fellows at other institutions do likewise).
