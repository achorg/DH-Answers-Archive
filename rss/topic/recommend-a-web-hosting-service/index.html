So one possible alternative would be to split up the hosting and the storage. You can get up to 1TB of Amazon S3 storage for a penny a GB, meaning you'd only be paying 57 cents a month with your needs ... then integrate that with the $5/month plan at Digital Ocean (where you don't end up paying for all the features you'll never use on a shared host ... they just spin up a virtual server for you to do with as you please), pay $12 / year for the domain name through a registrar-only service, and you've hit your price point. </p> scottkleinman on "Recommend a web hosting service" http://digitalhumanities.org/answers/topic/recommend-a-web-hosting-service#post-2304 Wed, 18 Mar 2015 12:34:17 +0000 scottkleinman 2304@http://digitalhumanities.org/answers/ <p>I've used <a href="https://www.justhost.com/hosting-features">JustHost</a> for several years and have been happy with their service and customer service.
