So, well done!</p> <p>On the principle that no good deed should go unpunished, however, it would be great to see it expanded a bit. In particular, a section on transformations, or on using the TEI stylesheet templates, would be a terrific addition. This stage in the process of text encoding remains, I think, the greatest bottleneck to new users. TEI XML is not particularly complicated and is easy to teach; XSLT, on the other hand, can be very difficult, and the TEI stylesheets are not presented in a particularly "user-friendly" way. It would be enormously beneficial to TEI noobies to have a resource that answered the inevitable question: "Ok, I have a tagged text.
