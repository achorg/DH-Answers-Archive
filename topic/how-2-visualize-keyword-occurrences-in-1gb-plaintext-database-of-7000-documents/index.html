    As part of my intellectual process I digitize (scan/OCR) all textual documents of use in my research and compile them into a focused text database which I mine using boolean searches and regular expressions.

    Over the course of five years I have accumulated over 1.0 GB of plain text comprised of over 7,000 full-text documents all of which are related to my specialization: Posthuman Studies.

    Specifically, what I'm looking for is a stand-alone visual mapping tool that can spatially link key terms, their synonyms and antonyms, etc., in real-time for my entire text database of 7,000 items.

    Thanks in advance,

    Chris.

When you do a search with that desktop federated search product, it produces an interactive keyword visualization that helps go through the results set. I usually do a screen grab on that graphic.
