  6. joncgoodwin@gmail.com
    Member

    I'd recommend using the word frequency lists, though you have to convert them using the method I described in the post you linked to in your original question. These lists are more reliable, I believe, than text extracted from PDFs would be, and smaller and easier to manage.

    I also believe that the word frequency lists do a better job of removing roman numerals and publishing abbreviations, though many of those do remain. In the models I've run, OCR artifacts have tended to cluster into their own topic, which make them easy to ignore. Is that happening with yours?
