  4. Joe Wicentowski
    Member

    You're right, oXygen only comes with FOP. Other than FOP most FO processors are commercial. It's too bad that even the recently-released FOP 1.0 doesn't support floats (http://xmlgraphics.apache.org/fop/compliance.html#fo-object-float). The two commercial processors that keep coming up are XEP and Antenna House. XEP's float support is described at http://mediawiki.renderx.com/index.php/XEP_User_Guide/Appendix_A_XSL-FO_Conformance#Floats_Alignment. Some samples of Antenna House's float support can be viewed at http://www.antennahouse.com/XSLsample/FOsample.htm.
