    I'll second the recommendation for Jing, which we use all the time, with 2 caveats:
    - it is for short screencasts (5 min.)
    - the free version sends your .swf files to screencast.com, facebook, etc. If you want to save as mp4 to your own machine, spring for the $15 dollar Pro version.
